🦞 Top 5 Buffets in Las Vegas 2026

Ranked by overall value and experience. Heads up — Vegas buffets ain't $5 anymore, but these are the best options!

1

⭐ Wynn Buffet — The Best!

Wynn Hotel • North Strip (near SAHARA!)

★★★★★ 5/5

The undisputed champion. Food is prepared in small batches for superior quality — a huge difference from other buffets. Lobster, crab legs, Wagyu beef, prime rib, caviar, and incredible desserts. Price has NOT gone up this year!

2

Bakanal Buffet — Biggest in Vegas

Caesar's Palace • Center Strip

★★★★ 4/5

The largest buffet in Las Vegas — 250+ items from 20 different kitchens. Crab, lobster (dinner only), prime rib, duck, sushi, and more. Can get crowded and hectic. Note the 90-minute time limit!

MealWhenPrice
BrunchFri / Sat / Sun$86.99
DinnerMon – Thu$86.99
Dinner (weekend)Fri / Sat / Sun$91.99

💡 Strategy: Go for most expensive items first (lobster, crab, beef). Make reservations or go off-peak to avoid long lines. 90-minute limit — move fast!

3

Anony's Prime — For the Connoisseur

M Resort • South of Strip (~15 min drive)

★★★★ 4/5

Pure elegance — the closest you'll get to the old legendary Sterling brunch. Prime rib, beef Wellington, lamb chops, roasted lobster tails. Sundays only — books up months in advance!

MealWhenTimePrice
Sunday BrunchSundays only11am – 1:30pm$99.99

⚠️ Book months in advance or show up at opening and hope for a cancellation. Worth every penny if you get in!
