💡 Cheap Eating Pro Tips

  • 🛒 Stock up at CVS or Walgreens — Beer is $3.99 vs. $16.99+ inside casino bars. Get water, snacks, and drinks to fill your mini fridge!
  • 📏 Portions are HUGE — Under-order! Split a meal and get a snack later. It's hot and you won't eat as much as you think
  • 🧾 Watch for hidden C&F fees — Some restaurants add a ~4.9% "Convenience & Facility" fee in tiny print at the bottom of the menu. Ask to have it removed!
  • ☕ Free casino coffee — Head to the sports book or center bar in the morning for fresh, cheaper coffee instead of waiting at Starbucks
  • 🍕 The "Strip Tax" — The closer to the Strip, the more expensive everything is. Walk 5 minutes off-Strip or go downtown for much better prices
