💰 Top Money-Saving Highlights

🚌 Free Shuttle

Downtown Loop shuttle is FREE and connects to the Strat → Monorail ($5/ride along the Strip)

🚗 Free Parking at SAHARA

Free parking AND free valet — a rare gem on the Strip!

☕ Free Casino Coffee

Skip the Starbucks line — head to the sports book or center bar for cheaper fresh coffee

🎫 Monorail Day Pass

$8/day for unlimited rides up and down the east side of the Strip

🛒 Stock Up at CVS/Walgreens

Beer $3.99 vs. $16.99 at casino bars. Grab snacks and water to fill the mini fridge!

🎵 Free Entertainment

Fremont Street has 3 free concert stages every night. Casino lounges have free live music too!
