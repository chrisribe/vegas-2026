🚌 Getting Around — Quick Cheat Sheet

🆓 Free Options
  • 🚌 Downtown Loop Shuttle (FREE → Strat)
  • 🚃 Tram: Bellagio → Park MGM
  • 🚃 Tram: Excalibur → Mandalay Bay
  • 🚶 Walking through hotel interiors (A/C!)
💵 Cheap Options
  • 🚇 Monorail: $5/ride or $8/day pass
  • 🚌 Deuce Bus: $8/day or $20/3-day
  • 🚇 Vegas Loop (tunnels): $4.25/ride
🚕 Ride-Share / Taxis
  • ✅ Compare Uber vs Lyft — always different!
  • ✅ Taxis: flat rate $19–27 from airport
  • ⚠️ Bring cash — cabs charge credit card fee
  • ⚠️ No cabs stopping on Strip — use hotel stands
