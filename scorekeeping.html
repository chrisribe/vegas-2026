📝 Match Night Scorekeeping Cheat Sheet (1 Page)

Built from official APA Team Manual pages for 8-Ball, 9-Ball, and Defensive Shots. Use this before every match so your sheet is clean and accurate.

✅ Captain Huddle (30 seconds before lag)
  • Who is top/bottom on our sheet? (lag winner is top)
  • Who is marking innings + DS this match?
  • Say it out loud: "Complete innings only"
  • Say it out loud: "Mark defensive intent, not outcome"

🎯 The Two Rules That Prevent Most Errors

1
Inning rule

An inning is not complete until the player/team listed on the bottom (lost lag) misses or fouls.

2
Defensive Shot (DS) rule

DS is based on intent (no intent to legally pocket). If no DS happened, write "No DS".

🎱 8-Ball: Exactly What to Mark

StepWhat to Fill
Before breakLag winner on top. Team #, player #, last name + initial. Fill race (games must win).
Every rackIn game block: innings tallies (complete only), winner block, E8/8S/8WP/8OB/BR, timeouts (T).
DS trackingTally DS in DS block all match. At end: total + circle. If none: write No DS.
End of matchTotal innings, games won, match points earned, running total, captain signatures.

9️⃣ 9-Ball: Exactly What to Mark

StepWhat to Fill
Before breakLag winner on top. Fill team/player info + skill levels + points required to win.
During rackCross out points as balls are made (1–8 = 1 pt, 9 = 2 pts). Record rack-end score lines.
Rack close checkTop points + bottom points + dead balls should total 10.
Innings / dead ballsMark complete innings only. Record dead balls; 9-ball is never dead (spotted).
End of matchTotal innings, DS total (or No DS), total points, match points, running total, signatures.

🛡️ Defensive Shots Quick Calls

DS
Mark DS

Safety attempts, legal contact only, intentional foul to improve layout, or clear no-pocket intent.

NO
Don’t auto-mark DS

Two-way shots (real pocket attempt + defensive leave) are not automatically DS. Judge true intent.

⚠️
Important

Even if a defensive attempt legally pockets a ball, shooter continues and shot can still be marked DS.

🚫 Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Negotiating innings with opponent scorekeeper.
  • Forgetting to write No DS when no defensive shots occurred.
  • Marking innings too early (before bottom player misses/fouls).
  • Missing special 8-ball marks: E8, 8S, 8WP, 8OB, BR.
  • Not doing a quick rack-end point reconciliation in 9-ball.
