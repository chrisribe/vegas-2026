📋 Format & Equipment

🎱 Tables

3½' × 7' Diamond tables — Simonis 860 Tournament Blue™ cloth · Aramith Tournament balls with Duramith™ technology · Diamond Tournament Cue Ball

🏆 Format

Modified Single Elimination — every team plays at least twice before being eliminated. Handicap-based (SL1–SL9), 5-player teams.

💰 Guaranteed Money

All qualified teams receive $350 National Qualifier Money just for participating. See Event Program for full payout details.

📝 Registration

Online registration opens ~2 weeks before your event (late July). Need your APA Online Member Services Account. Onsite backup: Mon Aug 10, 6–9 PM.

🎮 Side Events & Activities at Westgate

🎯 MiniMania Tournaments

Running 16 hrs/day throughout the event. Various formats, $20–60 entry. Great for non-competing teammates or off-hours fun.

👕 Team Shirt Contest

Voted online by fans. Show off your team's style — past winners had incredible designs. Worth entering!

🏅 Team Captains Championship

Captain-only event at Westgate. Max 5 players. Separate entry — good bonus competition for the captain!

🎰 Scooter Rental

APA partners with scooter rental companies onsite. Great for getting around the Westgate resort between matches!

💡 Team Tips

  • 📱 Claim your APA Online Member Services Account now — don't wait until registration opens in late July. poolplayers.com
  • 🏨 Westgate team advantage — teammates staying at Westgate can walk to the tournament room. SAHARA team: use the monorail ($8/day pass for unlimited rides).
  • ⏰ Arrive at 8 AM for practice on your match days — 45 min warm-up before 9 AM play.
  • 💰 $350 qualifier money — confirmed just for showing up. Keep your receipts and registration documents handy.
  • 🎽 Dress code applies — check the official site for current dress code rules before packing. See the APA page for details.
  • 📅 Check for schedule updates — this is the preliminary schedule. Confirm the final version closer to the event at poolplayers.com.
