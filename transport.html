💡 Quick Tips

🌡️ It's bigger than it looks

4.2 miles end to end. In August heat, walk max 1–2 hotels — use trams or rides for anything further.

🏨 Walk through hotels

Hotel interiors = A/C + faster. Example: walk through Cosmopolitan to reach Crystals shops.

🅿️ SAHARA = free parking + valet

One of the last Strip hotels with both free. Park here, explore by Monorail.

✈️ From the airport

Request Uber inside terminal so it arrives as you step out. Screenshot route — scams happen!
