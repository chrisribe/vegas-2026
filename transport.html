💡 Quick Transport Tips

🌡️ The Strip is BIGGER than it looks

4.2 miles end to end — 90 min walk in flat conditions. In August heat, walk only 1–2 hotels max. Use trams, bus, or rideshare for anything further.

🏨 Walk THROUGH hotels, not outside

Hotel interiors are air-conditioned and often faster. Example: walk through the Cosmopolitan to reach the Crystals — no stairs, full A/C.

🅿️ SAHARA has free parking + free valet

One of the last hotels on the Strip offering both. If anyone drove, park here and use the Monorail to explore the rest of the Strip.

✈️ From the Airport

Free rental car shuttles run 24/7 to the car rental center. Taxi flat rate: $19–$27. Uber/Lyft: request inside the terminal so the car arrives when you step out. Screenshot your route to watch for long-hauling scams!

🚗 Skip the rental car (unless leaving the Strip)

Parking costs $15–$25/day at most hotels. Only rent if you're doing day trips (Zion, Mount Charleston, Red Rock, Henderson). Everything on the Strip is reachable without a car.
